The Story Behind K. SAKTHIVEL

From a college student with a camera to a digital marketing entrepreneur

Journey from Passion to Purpose

Born and raised in Karaikudi, the creative journey started long before becoming a business owner. After completing schooling, moved to Tiruchirappalli to pursue B.Com in Computer Applications at SRM Trichy Arts and Science College.

During college days, passion for visuals was undeniable — capturing campus moments, creating engaging reels, and helping friends with photo and video editing. What began as passion slowly transformed into a professional calling.

Leadership became evident during the final year, successfully leading a major reels creation competition. Today, as Founder & CEO of Brandaura.inn, the mission is clear: helping businesses build strong online presence through creative strategy, professional visuals, and impactful content.

With trusted followers on Instagram, continuing to help local brands and businesses reach more people, attract customers, and grow confidently in the digital world.
